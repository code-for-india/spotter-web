About

You know what's wrong with your roads.
Show your city. Show your government.

Use this app to populate a map of civic problems in your city, free and open for public use and sharing.

"Everyone has a right to walk from one end of the city to the other in secure and beautiful spaces. A developed country is mindful of its people and its cities, because it is foolish to ignore one at the cost of the other."

“Elections belong to the people. It's their decision. If they decide to turn their back on the fire and burn their behinds, then they will just have to sit on their blisters.” ― Abraham Lincoln

We have grown with our cities, and it is our duty to help them evolve with us. This app was developed by passionate young individuals dedicated to the cause of urban reformation.
